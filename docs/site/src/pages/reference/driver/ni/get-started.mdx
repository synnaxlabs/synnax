The Synnax Driver supports reading and writing data from
[National Instruments (NI)](https://www.ni.com/en-us.html) hardware, including
CompactDAQ, CompactRIO, and PXI systems. This page will walk you through the steps to
start acquiring data from your NI devices using the Driver.

<Divider.Divider x />

## Installing NI-DAQmx and System Configuration Libraries

<Text.Text level="p">
  <Icon.Logo.NI /> [NI-DAQmx](https://www.ni.com/en/support/downloads/drivers/download.ni-daq-mx.html)
  and [System
  Configuration](https://www.ni.com/en/support/downloads/drivers/download.system-configuration.html)
  libraries must be installed for the Driver to communicate with your NI devices.
</Text.Text>

<Divider.Divider x />

## Supported Devices

**Synnax supports reading and writing data to all National Instruments devices that are
supported by the
[NI-DAQmx driver](https://www.ni.com/en-us/support/downloads/drivers/download.ni-daqmx.html).**

Aside from very old DAQ modules, Synnax is generally compatible with every NI device.
